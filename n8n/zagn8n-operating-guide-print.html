zagn8n Operating Guide

Daily operating guide for the ZagChain content pipeline: one strong content pack daily, review, approval, design handoff, then manual posting.

Final Goal

  1. Generate one strong content pack daily.
  2. Send it for review.
  3. Move approved items to the design queue.
  4. Keep final posting manual.

Main Workflows

zagn8n-marketing-ai-workflow

  • Reads ZagChain marketing data
  • Generates one content pack
  • Sends Telegram preview
  • Logs to zagn8n_ai_content
  • Forwards to approval review

zagn8n-approval-review-workflow

  • Receives generated content
  • Sends review alert
  • Stores row in zagn8n_approval_queue

Publisher With Status Update

  • Reads approved content
  • Writes to zagn8n_publish_queue
  • Updates approval row to queued_for_publish

Safe Canva Handoff

  • Processes one row at a time
  • Writes to zagn8n_design_queue
  • Updates approval row to design_in_progress

Main Sheets

zagn8n_ai_content

Generated content log and AI output archive.

zagn8n_approval_queue

Review, approval, and handoff control center.

zagn8n_publish_queue

Posting-ready queue and final posting tracking.

zagn8n_design_queue

Design-ready records and Canva brief tracking.

Status Flow

Approval Queue

pending_review approved queued_for_publish design_in_progress

Design Queue

queued_for_design designed

Publish Queue

queued posted

Daily Routine

  1. Let the marketing AI workflow generate one content pack.
  2. Review the Telegram preview and the row in zagn8n_approval_queue.
  3. If it is good, set approval_status = approved.
  4. The publisher workflow moves it into zagn8n_publish_queue.
  5. The safe Canva handoff workflow moves it into zagn8n_design_queue.
  6. After the design is ready, mark the design row as designed.
  7. After manual posting, mark the publish row as posted.

Recommended Schedules

  • zagn8n-marketing-ai-workflow: once daily at 10:00 AM Dubai time
  • Publisher with status update: every 1 hour or manual while tuning
  • Safe Canva handoff: every 1 hour or manual while tuning

Operational Rules

  • Keep content generation automatic.
  • Keep approval human.
  • Keep final design approval human.
  • Keep final posting manual.
  • Use calmer schedules to avoid floods.
  • Use one-row safe handoff workflows where possible.
Quick check: The system is healthy when one content pack is generated daily, approved rows move forward correctly, statuses update cleanly, and Telegram alerts stay useful instead of noisy.
